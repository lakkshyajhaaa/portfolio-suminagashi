Partnered with research stakeholders to scope and ship a frontend for a research platform serving 100+ active users at Thapar University.

## Deployment & Usability

The core challenge was balancing rapid feature delivery with robust asynchronous data handling for academic research requirements.

- **User-Centric Iteration:** Translated raw user feedback from research stakeholders into prioritized feature additions and critical usability improvements.
- **API Integration:** Integrated heavily with backend RESTful APIs, implementing robust asynchronous data handling patterns to ensure UI responsiveness during large data fetches.
- **Infrastructure & Deployment:** Collaborated cross-functionally on production deployment, utilizing Docker and Nginx while balancing complex engineering trade-offs against tight release timelines.
