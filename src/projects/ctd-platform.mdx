Owned end-to-end delivery of a full-stack attendance management platform adopted by 3,000+ students and trainers at Thapar University.

## Architecture & Security

Built to handle high-volume concurrent traffic during peak attendance periods, the platform replaces manual tracking with a secure, highly-available digital workflow.

- **Fraud Prevention:** Designed a robust control for attendance verification using time-bound 6-digit codes (20-second expiry).
- **Rate-Limiting:** Implemented strict request thresholds (5 attempts/min per student) to directly reduce the known abuse vector of proxy attendance.
- **RBAC & Authentication:** Defined role-based access policies (Admin, Trainer, Student) utilizing JWT authentication and secure email verification workflows.
- **Data Visualization:** Engineered 15+ RESTful API endpoints feeding directly into comprehensive analytics dashboards powered by Recharts, ensuring stakeholder visibility.
